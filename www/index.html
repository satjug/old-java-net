Title: JRebel WebEx

To participate:

  • Join us at New Horizons for pizza and door prizes!
or
  1. Get Eclipse and Tomcat up and running.
  2. Download the material for labs 1 and 2 from javapassion.com.
  3. Sign in to the webinar by 6 PM: https://www3.gotomeeting.com/register/424002494. A headset with microphone is recommended, and GoToMeeting reportedly works on Windows and Mac, but only under Wine on Linux.

Free license! Also, if you're reading this, you can get a free 3-month JRebel license courtesy of the San Antonio JUG at http://www.zeroturnaround.com/jrebel/giveaways/?campaign=sanantoniojug.

Synopsis: JRebel is a small JVM-plugin that makes it possible for Java developers to instantly see any code change made to an app without redeploying. JRebel lets you see code changes instantly, versioning classes and resources individually and updating one at a time instead of as a lump application redeploy. When developers make a change to any class or resource in their IDE, the change is immediately reflected in the deployed application, skipping the build and redeploy phases and preventing an average of 5.25 work weeks per year in redeploys!

This meeting will be a ~45-minute webinar presented by the JRebel folks. It will be available at https://www3.gotomeeting.com/register/424002494. You can log in from home, or come join us at our regular location to enjoy pizza, drinks, and door prizes!

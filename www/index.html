How to participate

  • Sign up for our mailing list at http://groups.google.com/group/satjug. Due to an infusion of spam, you'll either have to request a membership or be invited by another member.
  • Come to a meeting! We meet at 6:00 the first Thursday of each month at New Horizons Computer Learning Center (map to the left). See the calendar below for our scheduled speakers and topics.

View JUG @ New Horizons in a larger map

Who can participate

Anybody who wants to learn more about Java and related technologies.

Why to participate

Learn cool stuff, network, and win some great prizes. We currently have the following door prizes at each meeting: one personal license for IntelliJ IDEA, a book related to Java and/or enterprise development, and a bundle of other goodies -- T-shirts, USB hubs, and other promotional material.

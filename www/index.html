We meet the first Tuesday of each month from 6:00 to 7:00 at New Horizons Computer Learning Center. Attendance is free, and you're welcome to join us!

Next Meeting--1 Mar

Grails

Title: High Speed Development with Grails

Synopsis: David Singleton and Chris Cantu of Rackspace will perform a fast-paced and code-driven presentation demonstrating the power of the Grails Framework. During the presentation, several applications will be written from scratch emphasizing speed of development. The following topics will be covered:

  • Grails Project Structure
  • GORM
  • Configuration
  • BootStrapping
  • URLMappings
  • Creating/Installing Plugins
  • Scaffolding
  • Templates
  • Views
  • Creating/Consuming Restful Services

How to participate

Larger map

  • Sign up for our mailing list at http://groups.google.com/group/satjug. Due to a profusion of spam, you'll either have to request a membership or be invited by another member.
  • Come to a meeting! We meet at 6:00 the first Tuesday of each month. Use satellite view on the map to the left for details or see detailed directions in a custom map.

Who can participate

Anybody who wants to learn more about Java and related technologies.

Why to participate

Learn cool stuff, network, and win prizes, including books from leading publishers and a free license for IntelliJ IDEA. Meetings mostly revolve around what our members are currently working on, and you'll learn about people, companies, and technologies being used in the area. We're part of several user group programs, through which we're eligible for lots of discounts and review copies of books. We also have various door prizes at our meetings provided by our sponsors:
